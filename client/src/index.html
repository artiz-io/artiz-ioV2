Token: AZX

The ARTIZ token called AZX will be an ERC20 token and will run natively on the Ethereum blockchain. There will be a total of 80 million tokens, that will never be increased.

Artiz will provide the possibility to use the AZX token for a 50% discount on trading fees, for advanced features. Collected trading fees will be proportionally divided amongst AZX holders.
